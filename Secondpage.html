What is Cyberbullying?

Mostly it's about posting the negative comments and harmful posts about the victim.It's also including blackmailing and editing the photos inappropriate ways

How to prevent?

There is many ways to prevent it. Mostly by using privacy tools and setting. Keeping your personal stuff private is also important and log out after if you use public devices

Harresment
